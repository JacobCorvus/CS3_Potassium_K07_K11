INFO

Kai was raised a soldier of the Tide Port Mafia, the first born son of the family. He was ruthless, groomed into evil, but after being scorned by his family for sparing a life, he defected. Now, he runs an illegal immigrant smuggling operation. He is a very private person, and doesn't like to talk about his past.

  • Height: 6'2
  • Affiliation: Tide Port Mafia (former)
  • Ability: Organic Matter Manipulation

BACKGROUND

He was raised in the strict environment of the Tide Port, where he learned the ways of violence and intimidation. His upbringing was harsh, and he was taught to be loyal and ruthless. However, his conscience began to trouble him when he spared a life, leading to his eventual defection from the organization.
