APOLLO SALLOW

He / Him · Born — August 23

“When loneliness came and you were away, oh they told me nothing new”
— Bastille

INFO

Apollo Sallow is the current Commander-in-Chief of PATRON Agency, and the older twin of Artemis Sallow. He is a powerful sorcerer who wields the ability to manipulate light, known as Solaris. Ever since the death of former Commander-in-Chief Polaris, Apollo has been instrumental in leading PATRON through various crises and has a strong sense of justice.

  • Height: 5'8
  • Affiliation: PATRON Agency [Commander-in-Chief]
  • Ability: Solaris [light particle manipulation]
