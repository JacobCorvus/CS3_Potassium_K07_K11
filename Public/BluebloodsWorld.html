Sanctus

Est. 1800s

Formed after the cold war between the URA (United Republic of America) and the Soviet Union. Turned into a free country where anyone from anywhere are free to go. Home of PATRON's main headquarters.

Sanctum

the city where the main story starts. Established in 1941 as a Jewish safehaven from the Nazis, it has since become a neutral, free city and an international trading hub

Tide Port

Established in the 1980s at the height of the stock market, Tide Port has become a bustling city of dreams where any person can attain wealth. However, this fact makes it a gathering place of criminals, inciting turfwars and comitting heinous deeds.

Athenia

this city rose from the ashes of Athens, forming a place where knowledge and intellect is valued above even gold and money. Legends speak of a magician so powerful that they turned into the nigh-impenetrable walls of Athenia upon their death

Smoky Mountain

once a Philippine dump site, now a floating city that carries beneath it all the people's waste and junk. It has been turned into a capital for the rich, using the garbage to manufacture goods and whatnot.

Area Zero

also known as the Godhold, the place where the legendsry duel between Polaris the Dreamer and terrorist, Lady Oliviere fought to the death. Their immense magical energy has turned Area Zero into a wasteland, warped by magic and brimming with monsters attracted to said magic.
